Sunday check-in — what to look at
Reach & Impressions
Where: Tap any post → View Insights → Reach

Reach = unique people who saw it. If impressions are much higher than reach, the same people keep seeing it. You need more Reels to find new people.
Reach dropping → post more Reels, they reach non-followers
Reach growing → keep doing exactly this
Profile Visits & Follows
Where: Instagram Insights → Overview → Profile Visits

High visits but low follows = your page isn't converting. Check your bio and pinned posts.
High visits, low follows → fix bio. Add booking link clearly
Low visits → content isn't making people curious enough. Try more behind-the-scenes
Saves & Shares
Where: Post Insights → Saves / Shares

Saves = booking intent. Shares = free word of mouth. The algorithm heavily weights both.
High saves → this is your boost candidate. Put $50 behind it
Zero saves/shares → don't boost. Find a better performing post
Story Exits
Where: Story Insights → Navigation → Exits

High exits = people left before it ended. Your first frame wasn't strong enough.
High exits → open with the most exciting moment in frame 1
High taps back → people rewatched. Make more content like this
Best post of the week
Every Sunday find your single best post from the past 7 days. Write down what made it work. Replicate that formula next week.
High saves + reach → boost it Monday with $50
Low everything → wrong content type for that slot. Switch it up
Get more traction on your website
1. Booking link everywhere
Your WhatsApp link must be visible within 3 seconds of landing — no scrolling required. Also in every bio on every platform.
IG bio: "🚴 Party bike rides in Nassau · Book via link 👇"
TikTok + FB: same link in bio
2. Google My Business — free
Go to business.google.com and claim your Pedal Paradise listing. Tourists search "things to do in Nassau" on Google Maps. This puts you right there for free.
After every ride → WhatsApp guests asking for a Google review. One 5-star review beats 10 Instagram followers for bookings.
3. TikTok as a search engine
People search TikTok like Google now. Write captions like search answers: "Best group activity Nassau Bahamas" — not just vibes. Say "Nassau" out loud in your videos.
Add website to TikTok bio · Nassau, Bahamas location tag every video · Say "Nassau" in the first 3 seconds
4. Tripadvisor listing
Tourists plan Bahamas trips weeks ahead on Tripadvisor. List Pedal Paradise as an experience. These are high-intent visitors actively planning an activity.
Free to list, 20 minutes to set up · Also consider Airbnb Experiences for cruise tourists
5. Mobile page speed
Most traffic comes from phones. Slow site = people leave before booking. Test at pagespeed.web.dev — free, shows exactly what to fix.
Goal: loads in under 3 seconds on mobile · "Book Now" button must be the first thing visitors see
