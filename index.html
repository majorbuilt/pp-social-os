What to check every Sunday
Open Instagram Insights every Sunday. 5 minutes. Here's exactly what to look at and what to do based on what you see.
Reach & Impressions
Where: Tap any post → View Insights → Reach

Reach = unique people who saw it. If impressions are much higher than reach, the same people keep seeing it — you need more Reels to find new people.
↑ Reach dropping → post more Reels, they get pushed to non-followers
↑ Reach growing → keep doing exactly this
Profile Visits & Follows
Where: Instagram Insights → Overview → Profile Visits

High visits but low follows = your profile isn't converting. Check your bio, pinned posts, and grid impression.
↑ High visits, low follows → fix bio. Add "Party bike rides in Nassau" + booking link
↑ Low visits → content isn't making people curious. Try more behind-the-scenes clips
Saves & Shares
Where: Post Insights → Saves / Shares

Saves = booking intent. Shares = free word of mouth. These are the two highest-value signals the algorithm uses.
↑ High saves → this is your boost candidate. Put $50 behind it
↑ Zero saves/shares → don't boost this post. Find a better one
Story Exits
Where: Story Insights → Navigation → Exits

High exits = people left before your Story ended. The first frame wasn't strong enough. Always open with the most exciting moment.
↑ High exits → change the opening frame of your Story
↑ High taps back → people rewatched. Make more content like this
Best post of the week
Every Sunday, find your single best post from the past 7 days. Write down what made it work — content type, caption style, time of day, day vs night footage. Repeat that formula next week.
↑ High saves + reach → boost it Monday with $50
↑ Low everything → it was the wrong content type for that slot. Switch it up

Get more traction on your website
1. Booking link everywhere
Your WhatsApp booking link must be visible within 3 seconds of landing on your site — no scrolling. Also in every bio on every platform.
IG bio: "🚴 Party bike rides in Nassau · Book via link 👇"
TikTok + Facebook: same link in bio field
2. Google My Business — free
Go to business.google.com and claim your Pedal Paradise listing. Tourists search "things to do in Nassau" on Google Maps. This is free and puts you right there.
After every ride → send a WhatsApp message asking guests for a Google review. One 5-star review is worth more than 10 Instagram followers for bookings.
3. TikTok as a search engine
People search TikTok like Google now. Write captions like search answers: "Best group activity Nassau Bahamas" — not just vibes. Say Nassau out loud in your videos.
Add website to TikTok bio · Use Nassau, Bahamas location tag every video · Say "Nassau" in the first 3 seconds when possible
4. Tripadvisor listing
Tourists plan Bahamas trips weeks ahead on Tripadvisor. Get Pedal Paradise listed there as an experience. High-intent visitors — they're actively planning an activity, not just scrolling.
Tripadvisor listing: free, 20 minutes to set up
Also consider Airbnb Experiences for tourist bookings
5. Mobile page speed
Most of your traffic is coming from phones. If your site loads slowly, people leave before booking. Test at pagespeed.web.dev — free, shows exactly what to fix.
Goal: site loads in under 3 seconds on mobile · Make the "Book Now" button the first thing they see
